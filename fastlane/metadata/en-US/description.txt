When you want to have the weather, you want it to be fast, all the information you need on the home page?

With Weathery, don't waste any more time finding information, or finding out what it means.
Weathery will search weather data directly based on your location and display it instantly!

Find in this application:

* Current weather, temperature and feeling at your location.
* The forecasts (temperature, weather) in the hours that follow.
* The forecast (temperature, weather, precipitation) over the next 7 days.

Weathery also supports the magnificent dark mode, but it is also 100% accessible for the visually impaired and blind.

Find more features a little later!

* Real-time alert (strong thunderstorm, intense rain, risk of tornado ...)
* Rain radar
* Map of temperatures, precipitation, clouds
* Air quality
* UV index
* Widget
* Weathery on Apple Watch, Mac and AppleTV
* IMessage extension
* And much more ...
